OVERWATCH

The Game

Overwatch is a game for PC, playstation, and Xbox. Made by Blizzard, it is just as good as every other game they make. Overwatch is an online multiplayer shooter with millions around the world playing right now.

Characters

Overwatch has 22 characters at the moment with one or more characters on the way. Here is the list of characters that are in the game right now: Lucio, Zenyatta, Reinhardt, McCree, Reaper, Roadhog, Zarya, Junkrat, Widowmaker, Genji, Pharah, Soldier:76, Mercy, D.Va, Winston, Hanzo, Mei, Torbjorn, Tracer, Ana, Symmetra, and Bastion.

Season 2

Season 2 of competitive Overwatch has started. With a new ranking system and a new map in Germany. New balances are out too. More balances are on the way.
